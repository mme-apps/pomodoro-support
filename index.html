Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pomodoro Technique work?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into focused 25-minute intervals, separated by short breaks. Focus Boost helps you implement this technique effectively.

How do I customize timer settings?

You can easily adjust work session duration, break length, and notification preferences in the app settings.

Are my statistics saved?

Yes, Focus Boost saves your progress and statistics locally on your device.
